The Esgaian featherfly is a subspecies adapted to the coastal and beach zones of the [[Geography/Esgaia\|Esgaian continent]].
# Etymology
Unknown.
# Morphology
This subspecies is notable for its size and coloration. The wings grow up to 2 centimeters longer than average featherfly varieties. The body and wings display a nearly black coloration.
