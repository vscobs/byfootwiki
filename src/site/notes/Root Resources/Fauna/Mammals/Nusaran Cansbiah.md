The Nusaran cansbiah is highly adaptable across most environments.
# Etymology
Possibly from Proto-Germanic habīgaz (“heavy, weighty”), from Proto-Indo-European keh₂p- (“to grasp, have, hold”). Compare Latin capāx (“capacious, spacious”).
# Morphology
Similar to the [[Root Resources/Fauna/Mammals/Midivian Cansbiah\|Midivian cansbiah]] in size and stature, the fur of the Nusaran cansbiah is significantly lighter, often exhibiting a light beige or off-white.
# Physiology
The light fur is likely adapted to reflect solar radiation efficiently.
