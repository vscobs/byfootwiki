A subspecies of the [[Root Resources/Fauna/Tryferosomes/Ghost Pamperva\|ghost pamperva]], the seabed ghost pamperva inhabits the same range but spends most of its time stationary on the ocean floor.
# Etymology
Unknown.
# Morphology
Similar in shape and color to the ghost pamperva but slightly larger. Flattened, soft body, thin fin-limbs, and ghost-white coloration remain.
# Physiology
## Diet
Relies on passive feeding of algae and small organisms drifting within reach.
# Behavior
## Life Cycle
Egg-laying, maturation, and lifespan are comparable to the main species, with no parental care.
## Social Structure
Sedentary.
# Ecology
Waits for food to drift within reach on the ocean floor.
