The hyone is a small flowering herbaceous plant endemic to the [[Geography/Icosachores/EAST NUA/Rigus Crown\|Rigus Crown]]. It is known for its sturdy, white flowers and its adaptability to the harsh environment of its favored region.
# Etymology
Unknown.
# Morphology
The hyone grows up to 20 centimeters tall with tough, dark green leaves. Its flowers are small, reaching about 1 centimeter in diameter.
# Physiology
Adaptable to harsh environments.
# Behavior
## Life Cycle
The hyone blooms during the late spring and early summer months. It has a relatively short life cycle, dying off by early fall.
# Ecology
It relies on wind pollination and on the [[Root Resources/Fauna/Arthropods/Hyone Featherfly\|hyone featherfly]] for reproduction.
