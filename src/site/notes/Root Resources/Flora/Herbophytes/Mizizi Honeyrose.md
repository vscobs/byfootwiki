The Mizizi honeyrose is a hybrid cultivar known by its fast-growing nature and sturdier stems and flowers. Itâ€™s a cross between the [[Root Resources/Flora/Herbophytes/Honeyrose\|common]] and [[Root Resources/Flora/Herbophytes/Black Honeyrose\|black honeyrose]] developed by Mizizi.
# Etymology
From English *honey* + *rose*.
# Morphology
This variant looks nearly identical to the [[Root Resources/Flora/Herbophytes/Honeyrose\|honeyrose]], although the petals are slightly darker. It features stronger, thicker stems and petals.
# Physiology
Thanks to selective breeding, it is more resistant to environmental stressors than other varieties.
# Behavior
## Life Cycle
The Mizizi honeyrose has a rapid growth cycle, blooming in early spring and continuing into the summer.
# Ecology
It is grown primarily through seeds.
