The world is ruled by the **Brylaric Calendar**.

The Brylaric Calendar has **10 months** and **2 season-end weeks** (the first having 6 days and the second shifting between 6 and 4, depending on the year).
## Years
A year in the world is approximately 321.5 days long. The calendar catches up by reducing 2 days from the Winter Week every 4 years (3 long years of 322 days and 1 short year of 320).

## Weeks
The months are divided by approximately 5 weeks of 6 days each.
## Seasons
**Spring:** Besis 1<sup>st</sup> – Curmel 18<sup>th</sup>
**Summer:** Curmel 19<sup>th</sup> – Yalen 6<sup>th</sup>
**Autumn:** Cossea 1<sup>st</sup> – Minea 18<sup>th</sup>
**Winter:** Minea 19<sup>th</sup> – Pelen 6<sup>th</sup>/4<sup>th</sup>
# History
The months of the Brylaric Calendar are named after the Ten Old-World Creatures of Brylar.
