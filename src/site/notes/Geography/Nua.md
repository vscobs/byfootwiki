The land area of Nua is approximately 181,000 km2
There are three icosachores in Nua, those being [[Geography/Icosachores/EAST NUA/East Nua\|East Nua]], [[Geography/Icosachores/SOUTHWEST NUA/Southwest Nua\|Southwest Nua]], and [[Geography/Icosachores/WEST NUA/West Nua\|West Nua]]. The icosachores of Nua are organized in cardinal regions in accordance to the names of the districts (East, Southwest and West Nua).
