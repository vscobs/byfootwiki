## Demographics
The district is the third most populous in Vileus, following Coultfale and Berra. The population is concentrated in the head city, [[Geography/Icosachores/VILEUS/Tirsa Valley/Tirsa\|Tirsa]], which serves as the primary trade hub for the area.
## Heraldry
The coat of arms of Tirsa Valley is described as:
> *Tierced in pale Vert, Tirsian-Woodpod, and Vert, a sentera Or seeded Tirsian-Woodpod within a circular wall with four gates in cross, viewed from above, Or.*

This heraldry reflects the district's local flora, specifically the [[Root Resources/Flora/Herbophytes/Sentera\|sentera]] flower and the [[Root Resources/Flora/Thamnophytes/Tirsian Woodpod\|Tirsian Woodpod]], as well as the unique four-gate architecture of the city of Tirsa.
