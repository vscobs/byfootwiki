Ascelacis **AS** is the northernmost icosachore of the [[Geography/Stemarcrusia\|Stemarcrusian]] subcontinent. It borders [[Geography/Icosachores/CENTRIVIA/Centrivia\|Centrivia]] to the south, [[Geography/Icosachores/MIDIVIA/Midivia\|Midivia]] to the west, [[Geography/Icosachores/NUSARA/Nusara\|Nusara]] to the east.
