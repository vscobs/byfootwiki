Southwest Nua **SN** is the central icosachore and island of the Nuan continent, being bathed by the [[Geography/Oceans and Seas/Sercene Ocean\|Sercene Ocean]] on the north and west coasts and by the [[Geography/Oceans and Seas/Swingsea\|Swingsea]] on the south coast.
