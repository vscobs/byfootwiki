**Swings**, officially The City of Swings, is the Head City of [[Geography/Icosachores/WINDSWING/Windswing\|Windswing]]. It has an area of around 88 km² and a population of nearly 40,000 people. The city is subdivided in three subregions: Downtown Swings, Green Swings and Outer Swings.
