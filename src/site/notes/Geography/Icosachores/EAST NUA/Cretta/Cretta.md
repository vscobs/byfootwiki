Cretta is a transitional zone encircling the southern edge of **Rigus Crown**. The landscape is a mosaic of rugged outcrops and flatlands, interspersed with patches of resilient plant life that gradually yields to the greener areas further south.
# Heraldry
> *Per pale Honeyrose and White-Hightear, a Crettic charmell dormant proper, gorged with a chaplet of white hightears.*
