The Nuan Plains are located in central [[Geography/Icosachores/EAST NUA/East Nua\|East Nua]] island. Although biological life is diverse, there’s a general lack of tall plants, such as trees or tall shrubs, which reveals a landscape consisting of grasses, wildflowers and small bushes.
