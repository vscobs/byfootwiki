Sere Entaler, also known as the Great Nuan Woods, is a district located on [[Geography/Icosachores/EAST NUA/East Nua\|East Nua]] island characterized by year-round rainfall. It encompasses thick forests with medium-sized trees and a wide range of other types of flora, fauna and other natural resources.
