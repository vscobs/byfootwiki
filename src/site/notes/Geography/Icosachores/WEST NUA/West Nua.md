West Nua **WN** is the westernmost icosachore and island of the Nuan continent, being bathed by the [[Geography/Oceans and Seas/Sercene Ocean\|Sercene Ocean]] on the west coast and by the [[Geography/Oceans and Seas/Swingsea\|Swingsea]] on the south and east coasts.
