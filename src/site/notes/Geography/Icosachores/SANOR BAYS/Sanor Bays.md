Sanor Bays **SB** is a southeastern Esgaian icosachore that borders [[Geography/Icosachores/AMAPOR/Amapor\|Amapor]] to the west, [[Geography/Icosachores/JUNDA/Junda\|Junda]] to the north, and [[Geography/Icosachores/TARRAN/Tarran\|Tarran]] to the east.
## Heraldry
> *Per fess Teal and Argent, in fess point six annulets interlaced counterchanged, in chief an eagle volant recursant migrant Argent and in base an eagle volant recursant inverted Teal.*
