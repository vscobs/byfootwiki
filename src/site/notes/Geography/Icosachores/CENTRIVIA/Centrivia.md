Centrivia **CN** is the center icosachore of [[Geography/Marcrusia\|Marcrusia]], bordering [[Geography/Icosachores/ASCELACIS/Ascelacis\|Ascelacis]] to the north, [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Colmetry\|Colmetry]] to the south, [[Geography/Icosachores/MIDIVIA/Midivia\|Midivia]] to the west, and [[Geography/Icosachores/NUSARA/Nusara\|Nusara]] to the east. Its coast, in the west, is bathed by the [[Geography/Oceans and Seas/Gatebay\|Gatebay]].
