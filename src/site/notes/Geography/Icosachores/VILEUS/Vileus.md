Vileus **VL** is an icosachore located on western Esgaia. It borders [[Geography/Icosachores/ROSCOURD-YALIA/Roscourd-Yalia\|Roscourd-Yalia]] to the north, and the archipelagos of [[Geography/Icosachores/COAN-SCHELL/Coan-Schell\|Coan-Schell]] to the west, on the [[Geography/Oceans and Seas/Schellese Ocean\|Schellese Ocean]].
## Heraldry
Coat of Arms:
> Per pale, the dexter barry wavy Azure and Argent, the sinister Or, in chief a salic mafera Argent leafed Vert and in base five annulets interlaced Azure.

Seal:
> Per pale Azure and Or, five annulets interlaced in orle Argent.
