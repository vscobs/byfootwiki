**Tirsa**, officially **The Walled City of Tirsa**, is the most populous and main settlement of [[Geography/Icosachores/VILEUS/Tirsa Valley/Tirsa Valley\|Tirsa Valley]]. It is a walled city where the [[Castle of Tirsa\|Castle of Tirsa]]. The city has exactly four entrances, one in each cardinal direction. Gate North leads to a road in the direction of [[Geography/Icosachores/ROSCOURD-YALIA/Yalia\|Yalia]], in [[Geography/Icosachores/ROSCOURD-YALIA/Roscourd-Yalia\|Roscourd-Yalia]]; Gate East leads to the mountain range of [[Geography/Zones/Tein (Mountain Range)\|Tein]] East; Gate South, also known as the Tall Gate, is the main entrance of Tirsa; and finally, Gate West leads to the mountain range of [[Geography/Zones/Tein (Mountain Range)\|Tein]] West, where there is a road that leads directly to the town of [[Geography/Icosachores/VILEUS/Tirsa Valley/Seade\|Seade]].
# Heraldry
The coat of arms of Tirsa is described as:
> *Tierced in pale Vert, Tirsian-Woodpod, and Vert, a sentera Or seeded Tirsian-Woodpod within a circular wall with four gates in cross, viewed from above, Or.*

This heraldry reflects the district's local flora, specifically the [[Root Resources/Flora/Herbophytes/Sentera\|sentera]] flower and the [[Root Resources/Flora/Thamnophytes/Tirsian Woodpod\|Tirsian woodpod]], as well as the unique four-gate architecture of the city.
