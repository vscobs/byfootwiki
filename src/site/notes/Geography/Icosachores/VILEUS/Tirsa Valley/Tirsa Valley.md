**Tirsa Valley** is a district located in the Icosachore of [[Geography/Icosachores/VILEUS/Vileus\|Vileus]]. It is named after the narrow depression in the middle of the mountain range of [[Geography/Zones/Tein (Mountain Range)\|Tein]]. It has a total population of approximately 21,000 people and covers an area of 3,000 km². The district's administrative center is the walled [[Geography/Icosachores/VILEUS/Tirsa Valley/Tirsa\|Tirsa]].
# Etymology
The name "Tirsa Valley" derives from the geographical depression located centrally within the Tein mountain range. The exact etymological roots of "Tirsa" itself remain unknown.
# Geography
The district is situated in the [[Geography/Icosachores/VILEUS/Vileus\|Vileus]] icosachore on the continent of [[Geography/Esgaia\|Esgaia]]. It is characterized by the mountain range of [[Geography/Zones/Tein (Mountain Range)\|Tein]], which is bisected by the valley that gives the district its name.

# Demographics
The district is the third most populous in Vileus, following [[Geography/Icosachores/VILEUS/Coultfale/Coultfale\|Coultfale]] and [[Geography/Icosachores/VILEUS/Berra/Berra\|Berra]]. The population is concentrated in the head city, [[Geography/Icosachores/VILEUS/Tirsa Valley/Tirsa\|Tirsa]], which serves as the primary trade hub for the area.
