Nusara **NS** is the easternmost icosachore of the subcontinent of [[Geography/Stemarcrusia\|Stemarcrusia]]. It borders [[Geography/Icosachores/ASCELACIS/Ascelacis\|Ascelacis]] to the northwest, [[Geography/Icosachores/CENTRIVIA/Centrivia\|Centrivia]] to the east, and [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Colmetry\|Colmetry]] to the southwest.
