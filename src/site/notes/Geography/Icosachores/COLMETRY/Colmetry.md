Colmetry **CL** is the southernmost Marcrusian icosachore, bordering [[Geography/Icosachores/CENTRIVIA/Centrivia\|Centrivia]] to the north and [[Geography/Icosachores/NUSARA/Nusara\|Nusara]] to the northeast. Its west coast is bathed by the [[Geography/Oceans and Seas/Brylaric Ocean\|Brylaric Ocean]] and the south coast is bathed by the [[Geography/Oceans and Seas/Anaric Ocean\|Anaric Ocean]].
# Climate
Colmetry stands on the arid zone of the planet beneath the equator. Its [[Region Codes\|region codes]] range from very hot and very dry (the interior of [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Killesteeth/Killesteeth\|Killesteeth]]) to hot and wet (the north of [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Cunda/Cunda\|Cunda]] and [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Halfmoon\|Halfmoon]]).
