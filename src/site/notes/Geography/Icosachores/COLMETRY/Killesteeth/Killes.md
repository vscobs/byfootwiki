**Killes** is the Head City of [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Killesteeth/Killesteeth\|Killesteeth]] (not be confused with [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Killesteeth/Belitesse\|Belitesse]], of the same district, which is Head City of [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Colmetry\|Colmetry]]).
# Etymology
The name of the city refers to the [[Root Resources/Fauna/Mammals/Killes (Animal)\|killes]], a large semi-aquatic mammal.
# Geography
Killesteeth is located between the other Colmetrian districts of [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Cippalania\|Cippalania]], [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Cunda/Cunda\|Cunda]] and [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Dybowle\|Dybowle]].
## Climate
According to the [[Region Codes\|region code]] **44 43 LL CS GD**:
- Hot, wet winters
- Hot, mild summers
- Lowlands
- Coastal
- Good soil drainage
