**Killesteeth** is a district in the icosachore of [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Colmetry\|Colmetry]].
# Etymology
The name of the district refers to the teeth of the [[Root Resources/Fauna/Mammals/Killes (Animal)\|killes]], an animal whose teeth were famously collected by [[Society/Houses/House Killes\|House Killes]].
# Geography
Killesteeth is located between the other Colmetrian districts of [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Cippalania\|Cippalania]], [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Cunda/Cunda\|Cunda]] and [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Dybowle\|Dybowle]].

# Heraldry
The coat of arms of Killesteeth is described as:
> *Sable, a killes’ head issuant from the dexter proper, jaws open and armed Argent, holding in its mouth an annulet Or.*

The [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Killesteeth/Killes\|killes]] is the emblematic creature of the district of Killesteeth, intimately associated with the city of [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Killesteeth/Killes\|Killes]] and [[Society/Houses/House Killes\|House Killes]].
