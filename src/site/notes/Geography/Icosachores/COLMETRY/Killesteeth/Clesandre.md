**Clesandre** is a town located on the southernmost island of the [[Geography/Zones/Killes Archipelago\|Killes Archipelago]] in [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Killesteeth/Killesteeth\|Killesteeth]].
# Etymology
Uncertain. May derive from English *[[Root Resources/Fauna/Mammals/Killes (Animal)\|killes]]* and Proto-Stemarcrusian \**andr*, meaning “to lose sight of”. Together they may mean “where the killes was lost”, or “the lost killes”.
