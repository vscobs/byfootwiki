## Heraldry
The coat of arms of Yalia is described as:
> *Sable, a tailed rupatile in annulo proper within two cafer branches forming a circle, slipped and leaved Vert, conjoined in base and open in chief, the dexter branch bearing six bundles of blossoms Sanguine and Tenné alternately from the base, the sinister with as many, the tinctures reversed.*
