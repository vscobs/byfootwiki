Esgaia is the largest continent (approximately 479,000 km2) of the existing three and the least explored. There are nine icosachores in Esgaia, those being [[Geography/Icosachores/AMAPOR/Amapor\|Amapor]], [[Geography/Icosachores/COAN-SCHELL/Coan-Schell\|Coan-Schell]], [[Geography/Icosachores/JUNDA/Junda\|Junda]], [[Geography/Icosachores/MIFLEUS/Mifleus\|Mifleus]], [[Geography/Icosachores/ROSCOURD-YALIA/Roscourd-Yalia\|Roscourd-Yalia]], [[Geography/Icosachores/SANOR BAYS/Sanor Bays\|Sanor Bays]], [[Geography/Icosachores/TARRAN/Tarran\|Tarran]], [[Geography/Icosachores/VILEUS/Vileus\|Vileus]], and [[Geography/Icosachores/WINDSWING/Windswing\|Windswing]].

The icosachores of Esgaia are organized in cardinal regions in the following way:
—   **Northwest:** Mifleus
—   **North:** Windswing
—   **Northeast:** Junda, Tarran
—   **East:** Tarran
—   **Southeast:** Sanor Bays
—   **South:** Amapor, Sanor Bays
—   **Southwest:** Vileus
—   **West:** Coan-Schell, Roscourd-Yalia, Vileus
