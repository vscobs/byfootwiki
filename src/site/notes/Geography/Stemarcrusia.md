**Stemarcrusia**, also known as **Stemarcrusia Island**, is a subcontinent of [[Geography/Marcrusia\|Marcrusia]]. The icosachores of Stemarcrusia are [[Geography/Icosachores/ASCELACIS/Ascelacis\|Ascelacis]], [[Geography/Icosachores/CENTRIVIA/Centrivia\|Centrivia]], [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Colmetry\|Colmetry]], [[Geography/Icosachores/MIDIVIA/Midivia\|Midivia]] and [[Geography/Icosachores/NUSARA/Nusara\|Nusara]].
