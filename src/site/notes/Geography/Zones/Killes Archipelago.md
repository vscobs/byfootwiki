The Killes Archipelago is located south of continental [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Killesteeth/Killesteeth\|Killesteeth]], in [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Colmetry\|Colmetry]], and consists of three main islands and 8 islets. Out of the three main islands, only two are inhabited, those being [[Geography/Zones/Clesandre Island\|Clesandre Island]] and [[Geography/Zones/Killeswatch Island\|Killeswatch Island]], which both only contain one town with the same respective names.
