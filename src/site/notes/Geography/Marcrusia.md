Marcrusia is largely unexplored. It is the least populated continent out of the three. Its land area is approximately 437,000 km2. There are eight icosachores in Marcrusia, those being [[Geography/Icosachores/ASCELACIS/Ascelacis\|Ascelacis]], [[Geography/Icosachores/BRUG/Brug\|Brug]], [[Geography/Icosachores/CENTRIVIA/Centrivia\|Centrivia]], [[Geography/Icosachores/COLMETRY/Colmetry\|Colmetry]], [[Geography/Icosachores/FEROSA/Ferosa\|Ferosa]], [[Geography/Icosachores/MIDIVIA/Midivia\|Midivia]], [[Geography/Icosachores/NORTH MARCRUSIA/North Marcrusia\|North Marcrusia]], and [[Geography/Icosachores/NUSARA/Nusara\|Nusara]]. The landmass above the three main islands on the north of Marcrusia is [[Geography/Stemarcrusia\|Stemarcrusia]], a subcontinent.
The icosachores of Esgaia are organized in cardinal regions in the following way:
—   **Northwest:** Ferosa
—   **North:** Ferosa, North Marcrusia
—   **Northeast:** North Marcrusia, Brug
—   **East:** Brug, Nusara
—   **Southeast:** Colmetry, Nusara
—   **South:** Colmetry
—   **Southwest:** Centrivia, Colmetry, Midivia
—   **West:** Ascelacis, Midivia
