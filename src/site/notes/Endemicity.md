Root Resources can be classified based on geographical range and endemicity on the following levels:
1. Zonal
2. Districtal (one district)
3. Polydistrictal (two or more districts)
4. Monochoric (one icosachore)
5. Oligochoric (two to four icosachores)
6. Polychoric (five or more icosachores)
7. Continental
8. Intercontinental
9. Global
